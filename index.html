Mao The Dachshund

Snow Day in Orange

The air was light and colder than anything I had felt in all months of my life. I could smell squirrels and the sent of fresh deer droppings in the air. As I walked I had to step over inches of snow with my paw landing with a crunch. The wind blew and carried with it the smells of decaying leaves and wet mud. I had found my place in this world. Silence in all directions except below me and the occasional snap of a twig as a sky rat rested itself on a branch. I had found my happy place. I will hunt for the ball now.

A dachshunds purpose is not to simply follow orders but instead find the order of the world. This means some times hunting and finding a single truth and other times it means following the only truths we know.
